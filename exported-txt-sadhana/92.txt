Live in Sadhana Forest
For the past few weeks I have been living in Sadhana Forest. I came only to volunteer and found that there was so much more to it. I became a part of a receiving community with amazing people from all over the world.
I lived in the middle of the forest, Iearned a lot of easy ways how to be a better person for our environment, ate great vegan food, enjoyed afternoon activities and met new friends from all over the world.
Even the tough work was satisfying.
At the end I extended my stays even longer!
