Sadhana forest
Sadhana forest is a special place where a community of people who believe in morals and values, the environment and water saving lives.

The lifestyle in the community is vegan (Also suitable for non vegan people)

Throughout the day you can volunteer in different places within the community such as planting trees, caring for cows and more.

Every Friday afternoon a bus comes out of Auoville for a tour that explains about the place. Then watch a documentary movie and have dinner together.

On saterday, many people from the area come to the tea hut and have dinner together.

A magical place that allows you to look at life from a different perspective than life outside
