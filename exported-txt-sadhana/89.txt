A step towards a conscious lifestyle
I came for a visit and stayed for 4 days. I loved my stay here and I'm looking forward to coming back for longer.
The people here are really friendly and supportive, there's great community spirit, good healthy Vegan food cooked in the kitchen. The sevas include jobs like planting, watering trees, cleaning, cooking, cutting fire wood, etc.
Sadhana Forest is sustainable in every way possible and great attention is given to all the aspects of the lifestyle here. It is a truly wonderful initiative in this time of climate emergency to create awareness and help fight it.
It's is a truly life changing experience and has made me reconsider, and live more consciously.
