Loving Community
Sadhana Forest gave me the opportunity to connect to both nature and the community living here in a very unique way.
After the daily sevas (selfless service) one gets to participate in various workshops, introduce new ideas or spend the afternoon in the neighborhood.
My highlight : the mudpool!

Seeing the forest grow day by day and being an essential part of this process is an experience I hope more people can make.
